Rules and Judging

Clear rules keep the competition fair and the ideas sharp.

Participation Rules

  • Team size must be 2 to 4 students.
  • Registration is free but seats are limited.
  • Every participant must carry a valid college ID.
  • Teams may use n8n, APIs, AI tools, databases, no-code tools, and custom code.
  • Core project work must happen during the hackathon window.
  • Open-source libraries and public APIs are allowed with proper credit.
  • Projects must be respectful, legal, original, and safe to demo.

FAQ

Quick answers before your team registers.

Is registration really free?

Yes. n8n-thon has free registration for all selected student teams.

Can beginners participate?

Yes. A pre-event n8n quickstart workshop will happen two days before the hackathon, and mentors will help teams scope realistic projects on event day.

What should we bring?

Bring a laptop, charger, college ID, internet hotspot backup if possible, and any API accounts your idea needs.

Can we form teams on the spot?

Pre-formed teams are preferred. If seats allow, solo registrants may be matched with other students before kickoff.

Do we need to use only n8n?

n8n should be the central workflow engine, but you may connect it with code, AI services, databases, forms, spreadsheets, and APIs.
